BROWS LAMINATION

N0.00 Per Head

At Olivanders Touch, we offer professional eyebrow lamination services to help you achieve your dream brows. Eyebrow lamination is a semi-permanent treatment that involves shaping and setting your eyebrows in place for a fuller, more defined look. Our experienced technicians use a gentle, but effective process to straighten and lift your brow hairs, creating a more structured and polished look. The treatment is perfect for taming unruly brows, filling in gaps, and enhancing the natural shape of your eyebrows. The results of eyebrow lamination can last up to six weeks, making it a great alternative to daily brow grooming and maintenance. Our team will work with you to create the perfect brow shape and ensure that you are completely satisfied with your results. At Olivanders Touch, we use only the highest quality products to ensure that your brows stay healthy and nourished during and after the treatment. MICRO BLADING

$19.00 Per Head

At Olivanders Touch, we offer professional microblading services to help you achieve perfectly shaped and natural-looking eyebrows. Microblading is a semi-permanent technique that involves creating hair-like strokes in the skin using a small handheld tool and pigment. Our experienced microblading technicians will work with you to create a customized look that complements your unique features and preferences. The result is natural-looking, fuller eyebrows that can last up to two years with proper maintenance. We use only the highest quality pigments and sterilized equipment to ensure that your microblading experience is safe, hygienic, and comfortable. Our team will guide you through the entire process, from consultation to aftercare, to ensure that you achieve the perfect result. Whether you have sparse eyebrows or simply want to enhance your natural shape, microblading is a great option for achieving beautiful, low-maintenance brows. Book your appointment today and let us help you achieve the perfect look with our professional microblading services.
